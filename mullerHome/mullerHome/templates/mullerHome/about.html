MULLER Ian

My name is Ian Muller. Born and raised in Luxembourg. I did my Bachelor in Computer Science at the University of Luxembourg. The thesis involved extending the dependency functionality of an experimental operating system that was the subject of a another students phd studies.
I then continued my studies at the University of Luxembourg to complete my Master in Information and Computer Sciences. My profile of choice was Adaptive Computing. The thesis compared Web Technologies for implementing a Web API, then implementing a Web API and comparing its performance vs a local solution. You can find my curriculum here.
